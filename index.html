About Me

Hi — I am Iliya Angelov Radulov, a researcher and developer based in Europe.

I studied Physics at Sofia University ‘St. Kliment Ohridski,’ Sofia, Bulgaria, where I obtained my MSc in Radiophysics and Electronics. Since 1998, my work has evolved from electronics to materials science, with a particular interest in experimental automation.

In recent years, I have been focusing on integrating modern data science and AI tools into scientific research workflows, with the aim of extending and supporting physics-driven work rather than pursuing standalone AI or data science applications.

This page provides an overview of my projects, publications, and selected personal work.

You are welcome to connect with me via LinkedIn, or to explore my code on GitHub and Codeberg, as well as my publications on Scopus and ORCID.
